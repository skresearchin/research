About SK Research.

SK Research is founded with the objective of providing structured market research and analytical insights across Indian equity and derivatives markets. The platform is built around a disciplined, process-driven approach that values capital protection as highly as opportunity capture.

Approach

The research approach.

A combined framework that draws on multiple inputs before evaluating any market opportunity. No single indicator or signal is taken in isolation.

The research process begins with structure. Price action and trend behaviour are read on the higher timeframes first, then refined on intermediate timeframes for entry context. Volume and relative strength serve as confirmation layers, not signals in their own right. Once a setup passes these filters, it is sized against a defined invalidation level, and the reward-to-risk profile is evaluated against the prevailing market regime. This sequence is followed for both equity ideas and derivative strategy choices, so the output stays consistent regardless of instrument.

  • Price action analysis
  • Volume confirmation
  • Trend identification
  • Relative strength analysis
  • Risk-reward evaluation
  • Market structure assessment

Philosophy

Research philosophy.

Three principles guide every research decision, every framework choice, and every published note.

01

Discipline Over Prediction

The focus is not on predicting markets, but on identifying favourable risk-reward opportunities supported by technical and market structure analysis. Forecasts age poorly; processes do not.

02

Risk Management First

Capital protection and disciplined execution remain central to every research framework. The first question on any setup is "where is this wrong" rather than "where can this go".

03

Process-Driven Approach

A systematic process is followed to evaluate market trends, momentum, and probability-based setups. The same checklist applies whether the idea is an index trade, a swing stock, or an options structure.
